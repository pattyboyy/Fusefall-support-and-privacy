How to Get Help

  • Ask a question or request support: email fusefallsupport@gmail.com and include the details listed below.
  • Follow-up happens by email: replies are sent to the address you use to contact Fusefall support.
  • Purchase and credit problems: if Apple completed the transaction but the credits did not appear, include a screenshot of the purchase confirmation and the approximate transaction time.
  • Refunds: Apple handles App Store billing and refund decisions directly. Use Fusefall support for missing in-game credits, sync issues, or account problems after purchase.

Support requests for Fusefall are handled by email at fusefallsupport@gmail.com.

What to Include

  • App version: include the version number shown in the App Store listing or installed build.
  • Device details: include the iPhone model and iOS version.
  • Account details: if the issue involves leaderboards or account state, include your public leaderboard name.
  • Steps to reproduce: list what you tapped and what happened instead of the expected result.
  • Screenshots: attach screenshots for purchase errors, missing rewards, or UI problems whenever possible.

Common Help Topics

  • Sign in with Apple: Fusefall requires sign-in before entering the app. After signing in, you will be prompted to choose a public leaderboard name if you have not already set one.
  • Credit purchases: Credit packs are sold through Apple In-App Purchases and appear in the in-game wallet after the App Store confirms the transaction.
  • Restore / sync purchases: Open the Account screen and use the sync or restore option to re-check unfinished or recent StoreKit purchases.
  • Leaderboards: Fusefall posts scores to global and regional boards. Your chosen public display name is what appears there.
  • Capsules and skins: Unlocked skins appear in Customize Balls and can be assigned to any merge stage you own.

Troubleshooting

  • If a purchase is pending, wait for Apple to clear it, then reopen Fusefall and check the Account screen again.
  • If Sign in with Apple fails, confirm that you are signed into an Apple ID on the device and try again.
  • If your leaderboard name is not set, complete the public-name prompt after sign-in before expecting scores to post under your final name.
  • If you suspect a score, purchase, or skin inventory problem, include screenshots, your public leaderboard name, and your device model in your support message.

Fusefall currently supports iPhone portrait play. Include your app version, device model, iOS version, and screenshots when you email support.
